Easy peasy Steak

Ingredients

  • Steak: 150-200g
  • Onion: 1 medium to large
  • Garlic: one or two pieces
  • Butter: 50g
  • Salt, Pepper, Ginger, Tumeric: as you wish

Steps

  1. put your pan on the fire and add some butter
  2. add onion and garlic and stir it to see their color turns yellow
  3. now add the steak and when it color changes, turn over it
  4. now add your spices and some water and let it cook
