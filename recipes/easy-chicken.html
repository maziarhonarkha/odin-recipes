Easy peasy chicken

Ingredients

  • chicken: 1 medium chicken thigh
  • potato: 1 medium to large potato
  • onion: 1 big one
  • salt, pepper, tumeric, ginger (as you wish)

Steps

  1. slice onion and add it to your oiled pan
  2. add spices and after color change, add chichen
  3. watch out when chicken's color change, add two cups of water
  4. after 30mins simply add slliced potato
